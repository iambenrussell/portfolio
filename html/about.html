✏️ A little bit about me

My name’s Ben Russell. After college, I spent three years as the product manager for a DFW tech startup. While I was there, I began creating wireframes and prototypes for our application. The result was a more efficient development process, clearer expectations between the CEO and the engineers, and improved consistency in branding.

That sent me down the design rabbit hole, including UI and UX. As a designer, my personal style leans minimalist, but I know that every project has its own individual needs. I enjoy studying design patterns, following emerging trends, and implementing transitions to create a seamless experience.

In my free time, I study web development, which helps inform my design process. Other than that, I love hip hop music, stand-up comedy, and strategy games.
